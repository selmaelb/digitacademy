The analysis project

Within the Master Digital Cultures

Digital technology lies at the heart of the challenges posed by the transition in our society. The goal of the Master's degree in Innovation, Business, and Society (IES), Digital Culture program at Nantes University is to train professionals capable of supporting public and private organizations in their digital transformations.

The Master's program is located in Halle 6 Ouest, at the heart of the Quartier de la Création on the Île de Nantes, surrounded by digital sector companies and schools of creation (School of Design, School of Fine Arts, School of Architecture, etc.). Halle 6 also benefits from the XPLab, a userlab dedicated to analyzing user experiences, and a manufacturing lab, both accessible to students.

About the project: The S8 Analysis project involves conducting data analysis around a societal issue, and then creating a bilingual presentation in the form of a blog post or data-driven narrative. For this project, we were given the topic of examining access to and use of digital technology in schools in France, including primary, secondary, and high schools.
